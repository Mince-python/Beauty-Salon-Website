About us

Welcome to Ustazy Unisex Salon, where passion meets perfection in hair artistry. Founded by the renowned Mr. Al iman, our salon embodies decades of experience and a commitment to excellence.



Mr. Al iman illustrious career is marked by numerous accolades, including titles such as Champion of Montenegro and Champion of ex-Yugoslavia, alongside countless international awards. In 2006, he was honored as the Best Juror in the World, a testament to his unparalleled expertise and dedication to the craft.



As a member of the Organisation Mondiale Coiffure (OMC) and the Central Europe Educational Director, Mr. Al iman continues to inspire and educate hairstylists worldwide. He is also the visionary founder of the Association of Hairdressers of Montenegro and Yugoslavia, as well as the organizer of prestigious competitions locally and internationally.

At Ustazy Unisex Salon, we blend innovation and artistry to bring you the latest trends in fashion and hair styling. Join us for a transformative experience guided by the expertise of a true industry leader.
